About Me

A chemist trying to make sense of the real world

I'm Arooj, a chemistry student who somehow ended up writing on Medium, facilitating workshops, and collecting ambassador roles along the way. The common thread? I like making complex things feel human.

Chemistry is where it all started for me but it's never really stayed in the lab. It shows up in how I write, how I ask questions, and why I genuinely care about making science feel like something everyone is allowed to be part of.

When I'm not studying or running sessions, I'm probably drafting an article at midnight or figuring out how to make the next workshop a little more real for the people in the room.
